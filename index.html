OUR STORY

Since inception, our goal has been to support design enthusiasts and interior designers, both big and small with beautifully made, highly sought after pieces. We at Sonder Living provide original concepts that give way to a balanced and curated life.

People often ask us where did the name Sonder come from?! and quite simply... the name derives from the Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows, which defines the word as 'the realization that each passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own.'
